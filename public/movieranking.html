Christal's List of Sentimental Movies

The Movies I Loved Throughout my Childhood

  1. Coraline

    2. A brilliant claymation film about a girl in peril. I love the storyline, as well as the massive artistic talent and effort that went into making the film.

  2. Princess Mononoke

    3. A beautiful animated film, which I admire for all the handpainted nature scenery. The storyline also has a great mix of action and romance.

  3. Willy Wonka and The Chocolate Factory

    4. A classic film filled with adventure and wonder, as well as a lot of sweets. I love the old-timey feel to the movie, the musical elements, and the characters.

Honorable Mentions

  • Shrek 2 (The epidomy of childhood comedy)
  • Spirit (The coolest horse ever and an amazing score)
  • Homeward Bound (The ending always made me cry, Go Shadow!!!)
